Contact Us

Location

Pittsburgh Learning Commons is located at Community Forge in the old Johnston School Elementary building: 1256 Franklin Avenue, Wilkinsburg, PA 15221. If you are visiting, parking is located in the building side lots off of Franklin Avenue.

Get Involved

Volunteer and Intern

We are continually looking for volunteers to support our programs and bring new opportunities to our youth. Currently, we are particularly interested in folks who know how to program or build electronics, or are eager to learn those skills. Please email info@pghlearn.org if interested. All volunteers must be willing to obtain PA clearances to work with youth.

Partner

Not only do we support youth to collaborate, but we love to do it too! Please reach out if you or your organization would be interested in a longer-term partnership, either financially or programmatically.

Employment

Pittsburgh Learning Commons is currently running on all volunteer staff, but check back in the future for listed employment opportunities.

If you have any questions or comments, you can email us at info@pghlearn.org.

About us

Pittsburgh Learning Commons is a 501(c)3 educational non-profit based out of Wilkinsburg, PA.
