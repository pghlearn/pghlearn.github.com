Programs

Pittsburgh Learning Commons aims to support a wide-range of programs for learners of all ages, 8-80, in gaining skills and knowledge across science, technology, arts, and civics.

Afterschool Programs

We are offering a series of afterschool programs during winter and spring 2019! Email us as info@pghlearn.org to enroll. And check back for updates on more camps.

E-Sports Team
Description: Youth will learn about collaboration and teamwork through playing videogames with a focus on becoming professional.
Dates: Session 1 Jan. 7 - Feb. 25; Session 2 March 11 - April 29, 2019
Time: Mondays 6pm - 8pm
Cost: Free
Age: 12-18

Intro to Computer Coding
Description: Learn to code in python and typing skills with Carnegie Mellon University Students (Teknowledge).
Dates: Feb. 5 - Apr. 4, 2019 (no class March 12 & 14)
Time: Tuesdays and Thursdays 4pm - 5pm
Cost: Free
Age: 12-16

Playground Design
Description: Co-design the Community Forge playground. Includes a field trip to Carnegie Mellon University.
Dates: Feb. 8 - March 1, 2019
Time: Fridays 4:30pm - 6pm
Cost: Free
Age: 8-18
