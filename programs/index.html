Programs

Pittsburgh Learning Commons aims to support a wide-range of programs for learners of all ages, 8-80, in gaining skills and knowledge across science, technology, arts, and civics.

Workshops

The PLC Youth Development Project is a workshop series dedicated to equipping youth participants with tools necessary to effectively navigate high school, social setting and the work environments. The project provides among other things:
- academic support
- tutoring in math and writing
- boxing and physical fitness
- conversations on current affairs and social issues
- public speaking and interviewing skills
- movie nights and visits to cultural centers
- technology and science activities
The project aims to build confidence, instill discipline, stir social responsibility and to ensure that students are culturally engaged and aware.
